## What is AI automation?

AI automation is the use of artificial intelligence to automate workflows, decisions, and communication ‚Äî without constant human input.

It helps businesses save time, reduce costs, and improve efficiency.

## What tasks can be automated with AI?

- Customer service (chatbots, helpdesk replies)  
- Lead qualification and outreach  
- Social media content generation  
- Email marketing  
- Report generation  
- Appointment scheduling  
- File management and CRM updates

## What are the risks of AI automation?

- Poorly trained models can give bad results  
- Automating broken processes amplifies errors  
- Data privacy concerns (especially with client info)

The key is to **start small, test thoroughly**, and train your team.

---

## Which tools should I start with?

- **Zapier** or **Make** for basic task automation  
- **ChatGPT API** or **Tidio** for chat automation  
- **Instantly**, **Lemlist**, or **Mailchimp** for outreach  
- **WebNexaAI Custom Workflows** for tailored systems

---

## Do I need coding skills?

Not necessarily. Many modern tools are **no-code** or **low-code**, meaning you can create powerful automations without writing code.

But for complex workflows, partnering with an agency like WebNexaAI helps.

---

## How do I get started?

1. Identify repetitive tasks your team does weekly  
2. List tools you already use (CRM, email, calendar, etc.)  
3. Pick one workflow to automate  
4. Measure time saved and errors reduced  
5. Expand based on results
